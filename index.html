Doctoral Researcher at UCLA

Email CV Google Scholar LinkedIn

I am a PhD candidate co-advised by professor Daniel Tward and professor Eleazar Eskin at UCLA. I develop statistical and generative AI models for longitudinal disease modeling, specializing in generative AI vision models for multi-modal dataset including medical imaging and EHR. My work has been published in journals including Neuroinformatics, ESC and JMIR; and conferences including MICCAI, AAIC and ICML.

I have 5+ years experience in programming(Python, SQL, C++), and machine learning models(regression, supervised DL, transformer, VAEs). My work is driven by a commitment to transforming patient care and enabling personalized medicine through AI. Always open to collaboration and discussion on pushing the boundaries of AI in healthcare!
